Here is **Aibo Yao**. My research interests include but are not limited to: operations management, supply chain, blockchain, and platform governance. I am from Guangzhou and am currently a third-year graduate student majoring in Logistics Engineering and Management at Wuhan University of Science and Technology. You can contact me via my academic email: **yaoaibo@wust.edu.cn**.
